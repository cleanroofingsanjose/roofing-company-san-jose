Signs You Need Roof Repair

Not all roof problems are obvious. If you notice any of the following, it may be time to call a professional residential roofing contractor:

Internal Signs

  • Water stains on ceilings or walls
  • Mold or mildew growth
  • Rising energy bills

External Signs

  • Curling or cracked shingles
  • Granules in gutters
  • Visible sagging or soft spots

Don't Wait

Addressing these issues early can prevent structural damage and save thousands in future repairs.
