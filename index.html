Roofing Types We Specialize In

At Clean Roofing, we work with a wide range of roofing systems to match your property style, performance requirements, and budget.

Flat Roof

Ideal for many commercial buildings and modern homes, flat roofing systems offer durability and easy maintenance. We install and repair high-performance flat roofing systems designed for proper drainage and long-term reliability.

Metal Roof

Metal roofs provide excellent longevity and energy efficiency. They are resistant to harsh weather conditions and require minimal maintenance, making them a smart long-term investment.

Composite Roof

Composite shingles are a popular option for homeowners looking for durability, versatility, and affordability. They provide excellent weather resistance and attractive design options.

Cement Tile Roof

Cement tile roofing offers superior durability and a distinctive architectural appearance. It is ideal for properties looking for both strength and aesthetic appeal.

Wood Shake Roof

Wood shake roofing delivers a natural, timeless look while providing strong insulation properties. Our team ensures proper installation to maximize performance and lifespan.
